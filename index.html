FAQ

Common questions.

How does the setup work? +

MyGCTracker handles everything. After you sign up, you'll get a personal Telegram bot set up, a live dashboard for your project, and full onboarding — usually within 24 hours. You don't need to install anything.

Does it work for handwritten receipts and checks? +

Yes. The AI handles printed receipts, handwritten notes, and checks. If the amount isn't clear, you'll get a prompt to confirm or correct it before it's saved.

Can I track multiple job sites? +

Yes — the Pro plan covers 2–6 job sites and the Enterprise plan covers unlimited. Each project gets its own dashboard, its own receipt log, and its own spending breakdown.

Is my data secure? +

Yes. Receipt photos are stored privately in the cloud — only you can access them. Your dashboard is password protected and your expense data lives in your own private Google Sheet.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel any time and you keep all your data.
